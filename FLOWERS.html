Meet John Okello: A Successful Ugandan Farmer
John Okello started his journey with just a few acres of maize and tomatoes in Lira. Through dedication, smart use of hybrid seeds, and sustainable farming practices, he transformed his small farm into a thriving agribusiness. Today, John supplies fresh produce to major markets and inspires other farmers to adopt modern techniques.

"Farming is not just a job, it's a way of life. With the right tools and knowledge, anyone can succeed," says John.

His story is a testament to the power of innovation and hard work in agriculture. From drip irrigation to organic fertilizers, John continues to invest in new methods to improve yields and quality. His advice to fellow farmers: "Never stop learning, and always share your success with others."
